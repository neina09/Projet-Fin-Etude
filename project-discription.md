

### 1. App Overview
**Ommalak (عمالك)** is a localized digital marketplace designed specifically for Mauritania. Its primary purpose is to seamlessly connect individuals who need specific jobs or tasks done with verified local workers and professionals. The platform is inherently bilingual, supporting Arabic (with a native right-to-left interface) as the primary language, alongside English. 

### 2. User Roles & Hierarchy
The platform operates on a role-based system to dictate what actions a person can take:

*   **Standard User (Client):** Can browse the directory of workers, post open tasks to a public board, send direct booking requests to specific workers, manage their personal profile, and leave ratings/reviews for completed jobs.
*   **Worker:** Inherits all abilities of a standard user, but can also upgrade their account to a professional profile. They can set their availability (Available/Busy), specify their profession and pricing, receive direct booking requests, browse open tasks to send offers, and accept or decline jobs.
*   **Administrator:** Holds full oversight of the platform. They are responsible for verifying worker identities (approving or rejecting professional profiles), moderating tasks, managing users, and monitoring platform statistics through an administrative dashboard.

### 3. Core Features & Capabilities

#### Authentication & Account Security
*   **Phone-Based Login:** Users register, log in, and verify their identity using their local phone numbers.
*   **SMS Verification:** Account creation and password resets are secured via 6-digit codes sent directly via SMS.
*   **Seamless Upgrades:** A standard user can easily apply to "Become a Worker" by submitting additional details and identity documents for administrative review.

#### Task Management & Bidding (Problem Board)
*   **Task Creation:** Users can post a detailed description of a job they need done, specifying the required profession and pinpointing the exact location on an interactive map.
*   **Offer System:** Verified workers can browse these open tasks and submit offers to the task creator. 
*   **Selection Process:** The task creator can review all incoming offers from different workers, view their profiles, and select the best fit for the job.
*   **Lifecycle Tracking:** Tasks are tracked through various statuses (e.g., Pending Review, Open, In Progress, Completed, Cancelled).

#### Worker Directory & Direct Booking
*   **Search & Filter:** Users can browse a comprehensive directory of verified workers, filtering them by profession, location, and current availability.
*   **Detailed Profiles:** Every worker has a public profile displaying their skills, verified identity status, location, salary expectations, and overall user rating.
*   **Direct Bookings:** If a user finds a worker they like, they can bypass the public task board and send a direct booking request, which the worker can either accept or reject.

#### Trust & Moderation System
*   **Identity Verification:** Workers must submit identity documents that administrators manually review before granting them a "Verified" badge, ensuring trust and safety on the platform.
*   **Ratings & Reviews:** After a job is completed, clients can rate the worker (via a star system) and leave written feedback. This builds the worker's reputation score and helps future clients make informed decisions.

#### Real-Time Communication & Alerts
*   **Notification Engine:** The platform features a robust notification system that alerts users to important events—such as when a worker accepts a booking, when a new offer is made on a task, or when an administrator approves a worker profile. 

#### Administrative Oversight
*   **Admin Dashboard:** Platform operators have access to a secure dashboard that provides a birds-eye view of the platform's health. They can view charts and statistics regarding task volume, worker registrations, and active bookings, allowing them to manage the community effectively.
